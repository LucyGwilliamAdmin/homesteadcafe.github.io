About The Homestead Café

With a family run team of 3 friendly and experienced staff members, the Homestead Café has a professional team, qualified in all necessary hygiene qualifications and also staff with city and guilds catering qualifications and with over 30 years catering experience between us. We also over an outside catering buffet service which is rapidly increasing in popularity.See our page reviews and contact us for further details and requirements.

The café aims to please all customers with a range of meals, snacks and drinks and has a 4 star hygiene rating. The friendly staff offer customers traditional, home-made fare.
