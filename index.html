The Homestead Café

The Homestead Café is a café based in the heart of Brynmawr next to the Market Hall Cinema.

The café have a very varied menu serving a variety of breakfasts, main meals, light meals, paninis, baguettes, rolls and sandwiches. As well as this the café offers daily specials including traditional roast dinners, pasta based dishes and home made pies and a wide range of hot and cold drinks, chocolates, cakes and desserts.

All food is available to eat in or take-away.
